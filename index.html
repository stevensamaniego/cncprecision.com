WHO WE ARE

CNC Precision Machines International, LLC was established in 2000 in El Paso, Texas. With over 35 years of experience in the maquiladora industry, our team understands the unique manufacturing needs of the US-Mexico border region.

We are a full-service distributor of new industrial machine tools, providing sales, service, maintenance, and technical support for both CNC and conventional machinery.
