About CNC Precision

Your Trusted Partner in Machine Tool Solutions

With over 25 years of experience in machine tool sales and service, CNC Precision Machines International has been the go-to source for manufacturers across the U.S.–Mexico border region. Founded in 2000 and headquartered in El Paso, Texas, we specialize in serving the maquiladora industry and precision manufacturing operations throughout the Southwest United States and Northern Mexico.

Our bilingual team provides seamless support for cross-border operations, offering expert consultation, installation, training, and after-sale service. We represent a carefully curated portfolio of world-class machine tool manufacturers — from Swiss-type lathes and 5-axis machining centers to EDM, laser systems, and 3D metal printing.

Whether you need a single machine or a complete production line, CNC Precision delivers the right equipment, the right service, and the right price. We are proud to offer financing options and accept all major credit cards to make your capital equipment investment as smooth as possible.

Our coverage area includes Texas, New Mexico, Arizona, and the Mexican states of Chihuahua, Sonora, Coahuila, Durango, and Nuevo Leon.
