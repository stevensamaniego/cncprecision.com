About CNC Precision Machines

CNC Precision Machines International, LLC has proudly served the manufacturing industry for over 25 years from our strategic location on the US-Mexico border. We specialize in machine tool sales, service, and support for the maquiladora and manufacturing sectors throughout the Southwest United States and Northern Mexico.

Our fully bilingual team provides expert consultation, installation, operator training, preventive maintenance programs, and responsive after-sale support. We represent the industry's top-tier machine tool brands and deliver complete turnkey solutions — from CNC lathes and machining centers to EDM, grinding, laser systems, quality control equipment, and much more.

Whether you are outfitting a new facility, upgrading your existing production line, or sourcing replacement parts and tooling, CNC Precision Machines is your trusted partner for precision, reliability, and value.
