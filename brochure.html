About Us

CNC Precision Machines International, LLC has served the manufacturing industry for over 25 years from our strategic location on the US-Mexico border. We specialize in machine tool sales, service, and support for the maquiladora and manufacturing sectors throughout the Southwest United States and Northern Mexico.

Our bilingual team provides expert consultation, installation, training, and after-sale support. We represent top-tier machine tool brands and deliver turnkey solutions — from CNC lathes and machining centers to EDM, grinding, laser systems, quality control equipment, and more.

Whether you are outfitting a new facility or upgrading your existing production line, CNC Precision Machines is your trusted partner for precision, reliability, and value.
