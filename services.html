Our Services

Complete machine tool solutions — from consultation to long-term maintenance.

What We Offer

We support every stage of your machine tool lifecycle.

CNC Machine Sales

We sell new and pre-owned CNC and conventional machines from the industry's top manufacturers. Our team evaluates your production requirements, tolerances, and budget to match you with the right equipment — and we offer financing options to make it happen.

Machine Installation & Setup

We coordinate delivery, rigging, leveling, power-up, and initial calibration so your new machine is production-ready from day one. Our team also provides site preparation guidance to ensure your facility meets the machine's requirements.

Preventive Maintenance & Repair

Keep your machines at peak performance with scheduled maintenance programs, calibration, and alignment services. When something breaks down, our bilingual service team responds fast — on both sides of the border.

Parts & Accessories

We stock and source genuine OEM replacement parts, EDM wire, filters, grinding wheels, tooling, and consumables. Our border location means fast delivery to operations in both the US and Mexico.

Training & Technical Support

Get the most from your investment with hands-on operator training, CNC programming assistance, and ongoing technical support. We're available by phone or on-site to keep your team confident and productive.

Financing Options

Upgrading your shop doesn't have to strain your budget. We work with trusted financing partners to offer flexible payment plans, leasing options, and competitive rates that fit your business.

The Border Advantage

Our location in El Paso, TX — with an additional office in Cd. Juarez, Chihuahua — gives us a unique advantage in serving the maquiladora manufacturing corridor.

Whether your plant is in El Paso, Las Cruces, or across the border in Juarez, our service team can be on-site quickly. We understand the logistics, customs processes, and operational realities of cross-border manufacturing.

This proximity means less downtime for you. When a machine goes down, we don't have to fly in from across the country — we drive over.
