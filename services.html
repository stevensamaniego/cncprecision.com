Machine Sales & Consultation

Selecting the right machine is critical. Our experienced team works with you to understand your production needs, tolerances, materials, and budget to recommend the best equipment.

  • Financing guidance

Technical Service & Maintenance

Keep your machines running at peak performance with our professional service team. We service both CNC and conventional machinery across all the brands we represent.

  • CNC and conventional machine repair

Parts & Consumables

We supply original manufacturer parts and consumables to keep your machines running. Our border location means fast delivery to both US and Mexico operations.

  • Tooling and accessories

Installation & Setup

Proper installation is essential for machine performance and longevity. Our team handles delivery coordination, rigging, leveling, power-up, and initial calibration.

  • Site preparation guidance
  • Machine delivery coordination
  • Professional installation and leveling
  • Power-up and initial calibration

Emergency Response

When a critical machine goes down, every hour counts. Our strategic location on the US-Mexico border means we can respond quickly to urgent service calls on both sides.

  • Rapid response for critical breakdowns
  • Service coverage in El Paso, NM, and Northern Mexico
  • On-site diagnostics and repair
  • Cross-border service capability

Training & Support

Get the most out of your investment with operator training and ongoing technical support. We ensure your team is confident and productive from day one.

  • Operator training on new equipment
  • CNC programming assistance
  • Ongoing phone and on-site support
  • Application engineering

The Border Advantage

Our location in El Paso, TX — with an additional office in Cd. Juarez, Chihuahua — gives us a unique advantage in serving the maquiladora manufacturing corridor.

Whether your plant is in El Paso, Las Cruces, or across the border in Juarez, our service team can be on-site quickly. We understand the logistics, customs processes, and operational realities of cross-border manufacturing.

This proximity means less downtime for you. When a machine goes down, we don't have to fly in from across the country — we drive over.
