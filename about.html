About Us

Your trusted machine tool partner on the US-Mexico border since 1998.

Our Story

CNC Precision Machines International, LLC was established in 1998 in El Paso, Texas. With over 35 years of experience in the maquiladora industry, our team understands the unique manufacturing needs of the US-Mexico border region.

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the best options in quality and price for their machine tool needs. We are a full-service distributor of new industrial machine tools, providing sales, service, maintenance, and technical support for both CNC and conventional machinery.

From our offices in El Paso, TX and Cd. Juarez, Chihuahua, we serve manufacturers across El Paso, New Mexico, and Northern Mexico — always close to our customers when they need us most.
